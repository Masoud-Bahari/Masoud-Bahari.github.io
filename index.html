About

I work on theoretical condensed matter physics with an emphasis on multipolar spin–orbit coupling, unconventional superconductivity, and topological/mesoscopic phenomena in multiband systems.

Research interests

  • Multipolar SOC beyond spin-1/2 (high-j manifolds)
  • Unconventional superconductivity and symmetry-based modeling
  • Transport, spin–charge conversion, and topological phases
